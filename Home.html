Welcome to the Quiz Platform!

Test your knowledge in various categories and earn points!

Game Rules:

  1. Click on Start to begin.
  2. You will be directed to a login or sign-up page. Log in or sign up to continue.
  3. After logging in or signing up, you will be redirected to the category page to select your preferred category.
  4. Once you select a category, choose the player you want to compete against.
  5. To select a player, retrieve their ID from the group chat and provide additional details on the player selection page.
  6. The quiz will then begin.
  7. Each correct answer awards 3 points to the first player who answers correctly.
  8. Incorrect answers will skip the question and move on to the next.
  9. Each question has a timer, which varies based on the chosen difficulty level.
  10. You can stop the game at any time, but doing so will result in losing the match.
  11. At the end of the match:
    • The winning player is congratulated and advances to the next difficulty level.
    • The losing player is moved back to the previous level.
